Sacred Three Shakthipeetam

Sri Kamakhya Lalitha Shyamal Varahi Shakthipeetam in Mandamarri is a radiant Trishakti center. Three divine goddesses grace this sacred land: Kamakhya (desire), Lalitha (beauty), and Varahi (command).

Sri Kamakhya Devi

Yoni Peetha, bestows fertility and moksha

Sri Lalitha Devi

Mahatripurasundari, Sri Chakra worship

Sri Shyamal Varahi

Boar-faced deity, removes obstacles

"Revive ancient Shakti traditions" – Founder Sri Yogi Sathish Guruji
