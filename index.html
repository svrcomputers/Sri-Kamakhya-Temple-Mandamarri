About Our Temple

Sri Tri Shakti Astalaxmi Mahankali Devalayam is a sacred place of worship dedicated to the divine feminine energy. Our temple is a beacon of spirituality, peace, and cultural heritage.

Established in 19......., our temple has been serving the community with regular poojas, spiritual activities, and cultural events. We welcome devotees from all walks of life to experience the divine presence and find inner peace.

The temple architecture reflects traditional design elements while providing a serene atmosphere for prayer and meditation. Our priests are well-versed in Vedic rituals and ensure that all ceremonies are conducted with utmost devotion and authenticity.
