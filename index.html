Sacred Three Shakthipeetam

Sri Sri Sri Three Shakthi Asta Laxmi Mahankaali Devaalayam in Mandamarri is a radiant Trishakti center. Three divine goddesses grace this sacred land: Kamakhya (desire), Lalitha (beauty), and Varahi (command).

Sri Lalitha Devi

Yoni Peetha, bestows fertility and moksha

Sri Shyamala Devi

Mahatripurasundari, Sri Chakra worship

Sri Varahi Devi

Boar-faced deity, removes obstacles
